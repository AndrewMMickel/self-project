Relations

Allies

Ciel

The leader of the Resistance and the person to find Zero after we gained intel of an abandoned factory near our hideout. The Cyber elf Passy sacrified herself to revive Zero when she was pursued by Neo Arcadian forces.

The Reploid Resistance

A group of Reploids that were labelled as Mavericks by Neo Arcadia. The leader is Ciel, and although they oppose Neo Arcadia, they mean no harm to anyone. Most likely these folks were labelled due to not being fiercly loyal to the tyranny of Copy X. The standard uniform is green with a red beret and metal combat boots, and the standard weapon is an old variant of the Buster Shot.

X (Cyber-Elf)

A hero from long past my own databanks and the Original X. He gave up his body to seal away the Dark-Elf, and despite that he continues to help Zero and Ciel in their missions as a Cyber-elf. When Zero first awoke, X was the one to give him his Z-saber.

Gray-zone

Sage Harpuia

One of the 4 guardians of Neo Arcadia and commander of the Strong Air Battalion. After first fighting against Zero when he first emerged, continuos circumstances arise where Harpuia decides to spare and even save Zero's life, culminating in fighting alongside Zero when the whole planet is under threat by Dr.Weil. The most noble of the Guardians and acts as their leader directly underneath Copy X.

Fairy Leviathan

One of the 4 guardians of Neo Arcadia and commander of the Deep Sea Squadron. Normally a peaceful reploid, she has taken a fascination to Zero getting into combat with him whenever she can. In Zero's third mission when the whole planet is under threat of being destroyed, she joins Harpuia to assist Zero in his fight against Dr.Weil.

Fighting Fefnir

One of the 4 guardians of Neo Arcadia and commander of the Scorched Earth Squadron. He's the hottest head of them all, so hot that we can't directly ask him questions cause he'll yell at us. What he do know is that he put aside his rivalry with Zero to help him defeat Dr.Weil when the whole world was endangered by Weil's space fortress.

Hidden Phantom

One of the 4 guardians of Neo Arcadia and commander of the Cutting Shadow Squadron. He acts as the inteligence corps for the guardians and Neo Arcadia. He was the one to warn the Cyber-Elf X of the fight between Zero and Omega, and helped to rally the other guardians to aid Zero. He couldn't aid in the battle directly due to his conversion into a Cyber-elf after his first battle with Zero.

Mother-Elf/Dark-Elf

The source of all Cyber-Elves. Her creation marked the end of The Maverick Wars as X used her power to wipe away the Maverick-virus. Weil corrupted her, turning the loving and gentle being into one that spread madness. Zero rescues her during his fight against Omega, where she finally turns back into Mother-Elf.

Enemies

Copy X

The leader of Neo Arcadia. He was created with the DNA of the original X by Ciel after the original used his body to seal the Dark Elf. He has 4 guardians that serve directly under him with the purpose of protecting Neo Arcadia.

Dr.Weil

A dangerous and cunning mastermind that manipulated ongoing events to his liking. He Was one of the individuals to work on Mother-Elf and the same one to corrupt her into the Dark-Elf. He also stole Zero's original body and used it to create Omega, a walking killing machine.

Omega

Zero's Original body taken by Weil and used for destructive means. There's a name that keeps on popping up whenever any of us look at it. Wily? Who's Wily? No records of him exist. As if he was scrubbed from existance.. This body was destroyed before we could analyze it, leaving only this image as the living piece of info we can gather.
